This file contains prompts for client outreach, lead conversion, and closing deals.

Use these when you want to get clients or start conversations.

Prompt 1, Client Outreach

Act as a business development expert.
Write a short and direct outreach message to a [type of business] offering [your service].
Keep it clear, confident, and focused on value.
End with a simple call to action.

Example use
Tourism company outreach for car rental partnership

Expected result
A clean message you can send on WhatsApp or email

Prompt 2, Follow Up

Act as a sales expert.
Write a follow-up message for a client who did not respond.
Keep it polite, direct, and focused on reminding them of value.
Do not sound desperate.

Prompt 3, Closing Message

Act as a sales closer.
Write a message to finalize a deal after interest has been shown.
Reinforce value and guide the client to take action.
