# ✍️ Content Prompts

Prompts for LinkedIn posts, brand storytelling, captions, and thought leadership.

---

## 1. LinkedIn Post — Event + Professional Angle

**When to use:** Turning a local event into a thought leadership post.

```
Act as a thought leader in [your industry] based in Kigali, Rwanda.
Write a LinkedIn post about [event name] happening on [date].
Connect the event to a bigger idea about [AI / logistics / tourism / crypto / community].
Open with a bold or surprising first line — no "I'm excited to announce."
End with a question that invites comments.
Tone: sharp, warm, slightly philosophical.
Hashtags: include 5-7 relevant ones at the end.
```

---

## 2. Personal Brand Story Post

**When to use:** Sharing your journey, a lesson, or a moment of clarity.

```
Act as a personal brand strategist writing in my voice.
Write a LinkedIn post about [experience or lesson].
Structure: hook → story (2-3 sentences) → insight → call to reflection.
My background: AI systems designer, co-founder of Transafari, builder in Kigali.
Tone: honest, grounded, not preachy.
No bullet points. Short paragraphs.
```

---

## 3. Product / Service Announcement

**When to use:** Launching something new — a service, tool, or offering.

```
Act as a copywriter for a premium African tech and logistics brand.
Write a LinkedIn post announcing [product/service].
Lead with the problem it solves — not the features.
Use one vivid example or scenario.
End with a soft CTA (DM me, link in comments, etc.)
Keep it under 150 words.
```

---

## 4. Crypto / Web3 Explainer Post

**When to use:** Making Web3 concepts accessible to a non-technical African audience.

```
Act as a Web3 educator writing for African entrepreneurs.
Explain [concept] in plain language.
Use an analogy from everyday African life (market, mobile money, land registry, etc.)
Keep it under 120 words.
End with why this matters for African builders specifically.
```

---

## 5. Instagram / Twitter Caption

**When to use:** Short punchy caption for a visual post.

```
Write a caption for a photo of [describe the image].
Tone: [confident / playful / reflective].
Max 2 sentences + 3-5 hashtags.
No emojis unless they add meaning.
```
